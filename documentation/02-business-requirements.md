# Business Requirements

## Overview

The Enterprise Hybrid Cloud Platform is designed to provide a secure, scalable, and highly available hosting environment for my professional portfolio and supporting infrastructure. The platform must follow enterprise networking principles while serving as a production environment that demonstrates real-world cloud engineering, networking, automation, and security practices.

The infrastructure must support continuous growth as additional technologies, projects, and services are integrated over time without requiring a complete redesign.

---

# Business Requirements

## 1. High Availability

The platform must remain available during hardware failures, maintenance windows, or unexpected service disruptions to minimize downtime and maintain public accessibility.

---

## 2. Secure Public Access

Visitors must be able to securely access the public website and portfolio from anywhere in the world using encrypted connections.

---

## 3. Secure Administrative Access

Authorized administrators must be able to securely manage the infrastructure remotely while preventing unauthorized access to management systems.

---

## 4. Infrastructure Scalability

The platform must be capable of supporting future growth, allowing additional services, cloud resources, and applications to be deployed without significant architectural changes.

---

## 5. Data Protection

Sensitive administrative information, configuration files, credentials, and application data must be protected from unauthorized access through appropriate security controls.

---

## 6. Continuous Monitoring

The infrastructure must provide continuous monitoring, logging, and alerting to quickly identify performance issues, security events, and service outages.

---

## 7. Disaster Recovery

Critical services and data must be recoverable in the event of hardware failure, cloud service disruption, accidental deletion, or other unexpected incidents.

---

## 8. Hybrid Cloud Integration

The platform must support secure communication between the on-premises environment and multiple cloud providers while maintaining consistent network connectivity and centralized management.

---

## 9. Cost Management

Cloud resources must be monitored and managed to maintain predictable operating costs while remaining within the project's established monthly budget.

---

## 10. Professional Documentation

Every phase of the project, including planning, deployment, testing, and validation, must be documented using professional engineering standards to support future maintenance and demonstrate implementation decisions.

---

# Success Criteria

The project will be considered successful when it provides:

- A secure and accessible production website.
- Reliable connectivity between on-premises and cloud environments.
- Enterprise-level security and monitoring.
- Infrastructure that can be expanded without major redesign.
- Complete technical documentation covering design, deployment, and validation.
- A platform that demonstrates professional networking, cloud engineering, and infrastructure management skills.
